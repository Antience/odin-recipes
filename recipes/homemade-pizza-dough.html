Ingredients

  • 1 package yeast (2 1/4 tsp)
  • lukewarm water 1 1/3 cup
  • flour
  • oil 1 to 2 tbsp
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • cornmeal

Steps

  1. Mix dough ingredients by hand.
  2. Knead by hand or mixer.
  3. Place dough ing greased mixing bowl, cover, set aside for 90 minutes or overnight in refrigerator.
  4. Punch risen dough down, Divide in 2.
  5. Roll into 12 inch circle. Cover and rest as you prep toppings.
  6. Top with favourite toppings.
  7. Bake at very high temp for about 15 minutes.
