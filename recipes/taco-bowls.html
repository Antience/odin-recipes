Ingredients

  • 1 - 1 1/2 lbs Ground Beef or Turkey
  • 1/2 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 pkg Taco Seasoning
  • 1 can Black Beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 1/2 cups Frozen Corn Kernels
  • 2 cups Salsa
  • 1 can Diced Green Chiles, drain
  • 1 1/2 cups Long Grain White Rice, well rinsed
  • 1 3/4 cups Beef or Chicken Broth

Garnishes

  • 1 cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1/4 cup Cilantro, chopped
  • Sour Cream
  • Salsa
  • Avocado
  • Tortilla Chips
  • Hot Sauce
  • Lime Wedges

Steps

  1. Turn on the pot's Saute setting and brown ground beef. Cancel Saute & drain.
  2. Add onion powder, garlic powder, taco seasoning, stir.
  3. Layer beans, corn, salsa, green chiles, & rice. Do not stir.
  4. Por broth over the layers, do not stir. Use a fork or back of spoon to gently press the rice down into the broth to sumerge it. It's okay if it is not completely submerged.
  5. Place the lid on the pot and set the steam release knob to the Sealing position.
  6. Pressure Cook/Manual button or dial, and then +/- button or dial to select 8 minutes. The pot will take several minutes to come to pressure.
  7. After cook time has ended, do a Quick Release of the pressure.
  8. Open when no longer under pressure.
  9. Fluff rice with fork and stire mixture.
  10. Serve with your favourite garnishes/toppings.
  11. If you are meal prepping, dish up into your containers and let cool before putting on lids and put in refrigerator.
