Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef, pork, chicken, or turkey
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • 4 ounces cream cheese
  • 20 jumbo pasta shells
  • 1 1/2 cups salsa
  • 1 cup taco sauce
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup monterrey jack cheese

For toppings:

  • 3 green onions
  • sour cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Brown ground meat. Add seasoning and prepare according to instructions. Add cream cheese, cover and simmer until melted. Stir well. Cool.
  3. While cooking ground beef, cook pasta shells according to instructions. Set out individually so not to stick.
  4. Cover bottom of 9x13 baking dish with salsa. Stuff each shell with meat mixture. Place shells in dish open side up. Cover with taco sauce. Cover with foil and bake for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Add shreed cheese and bake for 5-10 more minutes with foil removed until cheese melts. Top with green onions or olives if desired. Serve with sour cream and more salsa.
